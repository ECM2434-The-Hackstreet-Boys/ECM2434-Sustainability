Who We Are

  • ⓘ

    Our Mission

    We aim to make learning about sustainability engaging and interactive. By blending education with real-world exploration, our game lets you discover ecological sites on Exeter university's campus whilst testing your sustainable knowledge!

  • ⓘ

    Sustainability

    Sustainability is at the heart of our game. You will be able to explore key locations on campus, and discover the biodiversity we have here. Every question answered builds awareness and highlights the importance of small, everyday choices in contributing to a healthier planet.

  • ⓘ

    Our Game

    Our location-based quiz game challenges you to visit sustainability hotspots around campus, answer eco-themed questions, and earn points to buy flowers for you garden! Another way of obtaining points is by tracking your recycling habits by scanning the QR codes on bins around campus. The more you explore , recycle and learn, the more vibrant your garden becomes!
